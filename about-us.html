BLive Music is India's new music label with a current roster that
includes a broad array of local superstars. It is home to premier
record labels representing music from every genre. We identify and
develop recording artistes and songwriters, and we produce,
distribute and promote the most critically acclaimed music to delight
and entertain fans around the world.

BLive Music is platform for the delivery of music and related content.
It empowers innovators and allows new artistic opportunities to
flourish.Knowing that music, a powerful force for good in the world, is
unique in its ability to inspire people and bring them together, we
work with our artistes to serve our communities. We are the home for
music’s greatest artistes, innovators, and entrepreneurs.
